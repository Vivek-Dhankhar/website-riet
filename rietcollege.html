Courses Offered

B.TECH

Candidates should have studied a Degree course with minimum 3 years duration (after 10+2), with Mathematics/ Statistics/ Computer Science/ Computer Applications/ Engineering and Technology as a Main/ Subsidiary/ Core subject at the Degree level. The candidate should have secured a minimum aggregate in Graduate with minimum 50% aggregate for general category and 45% aggregate for SC/ST/Others in their Degree examination. Those who are pursuing final year bachelor degree are also eligible, provided they submit certificate of course completion at the time of admission.
